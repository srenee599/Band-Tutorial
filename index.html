Hello everyone!

I’m so excited to share this tool with you guys, as it’s something I found this year, and it’s been my saving grace for digital learning and online communication with my peers for group activities.

Band is a neat little platform that takes your group projects a step further by not only allowing you to chat quickly with memebers, but allows you to share a feed to keep one another updated and in-the-know at all times.

The first thing to do is to sign up for an account, and prepare for greatness. You can use your phone number, an email, or preexisting social media accounts to sign up.

Let's start our Band!

The next thing you should do is create your band! You have the option to pick the type of Band you want to make, name your Band, and set an image to represent your Band!

Wait... there's more!

In addition to that, you can also set up a band description to further let your members know what they’re in for when they sign up to your Band.

Let's get posting!

After setting up your accounts cosmetics, add some posts! You can add videos, photos, and polls to interact with your Band members, and they can comment, like, and even share their own posts if permitted. Let's try adding a picture first.

What else is there?

Posting photos or text doesn't always get the point across. How about some videos? You can upload from your computer, or you can link from Youtube as shown.

How about this?

One of my favorite features provided by Band is the polling option! Not only is this good for figuring out the favorite movies of your group members, but it also helps with creating a schedule for seamless meet ups.

It's pretty lonley in here...

After making a few posts, you might have realized there’s no one in your Band to see them yet…to make that happen, send out some invites! You can copy a link, send an invite code, use a QR code, or go through inviting someone else with a Band account to your Band (on Band)!

Talk away!

One of the hardest parts of group activities is consistent communication, but the chat feature is here to help! Once you’ve got some members, take some time to chat with them! You can send attachments like photos, videos, GIFS, documents, and even start an audio call.

All in one place!

Something a lot of groups need to do is share files: and that’s why Band has an option to link files directly to the page for all your members to view and interact with. Goodbye to tracking down informaton mention four meetings ago!

Let's hang...

Also, make use of the Event feature by inviting your friends to virtual hang outs, movie showings at your house, or even throw your birthday on there! Whether it's for play, or for work, Events can help keep everyone on track and connected.

Go find your Band!

Aside from being beneficial for school, Band has the option for you to explore other open forums as well. Whether you're into travel tips, films, books, or anime, there's a Band for you.
