Hello everyone!

I’m so excited to share this tool with you guys, as it’s something I found this year, and it’s been my saving grace for digital learning and online communication with my peers for group activities.

Band is a neat little platform that takes your group projects a step further by not only allowing you to chat quickly with memebers via group messaging, but it also allows you to share a newsfeed to keep one another updated and in-the-know at all times.

The first thing to do is to sign up for an account, and prepare yourself for greatness. You can use your phone number, an email, or a preexisting social media account to sign up.

Step 1

Let's start our Band!

The next thing you should do is create your band (group)...on Band (the website)!

Once you select "Create a Band" on the homepage, you'll have the option to pick the type of Band you want to make, name your Band, and set an image to represent your Band via upload!

Step 2

Wait... there's more!

In addition to that, you can also click "Set band description and url" under your new photo that you selected to represent your band, and further customize your new space by setting up a band description to let your members know what they’re in for when they sign up to your band.

Step 3Let's get posting!

After setting up your accounts cosmetics, you can check out your band's newsfeed and add some posts! You can add videos, photos, and polls to interact with your band members, and they can comment, like, and even share their own posts (if permitted by you, of course).

Let's try adding a picture first by clicking the little symbol that resembles a photograph.

Step 4

What else is there?

Posting photos or text doesn't always get the point across. How about posting some videos?

You can upload a video from your computer, or you can link a video from Youtube (like shown in the example)! Just click the little "play" button.

Step 5

How about this?

One of my favorite features provided by Band is the polling option! Not only is this good for figuring out the favorite movies of your group members, but it also helps with creating a schedule for seamless meet ups.

Check it out by clicking the little button that looks like a bar graph! You can ask a single question with the option of the answers of your choosing--and you can even allow your band members to choose more than one!

Step 6

It's pretty lonely in here...

After making a few posts, you might have realized there’s no one in your band to see them on the newsfeed yet…to make that happen, send out some invites!

By going back under your band's logo, you can click the little "Invite" button which gives you the option to copy a link, send an invite code, use a QR code, or go through inviting someone else with a band account to your Band (on Band)!

Step 7

Talk away!

One of the hardest parts of group activities is maintaining consistent communication, but the chat feature is here to help! Once you’ve got some members, take some time to chat with them!

Click on the little chat bubble at the top-right of the scrren, and you can send attachments like photos, videos, GIFS, documents, and even start an audio call with your band memebers.

Step 8

All in one place!

Something a lot of groups need to do is share files: and that’s why Band has an option to link files directly to the page for all your members to view and interact with.

Goodbye to tracking down informaton mentioned four meetings ago! Select the "Attatchments" tab towards the top of the screen, and get to uploadin'.

Step 8

Let's hang...

Also, make use of the Event feature by inviting your friends to virtual hang outs, movie showings at your house, or even throw your birthday on there! Whether it's for play, or for work, events can help keep everyone on track and connected--click the "Events" tab and give it a whirl by using the given calendar.

Step 9

Go find some other band's on Band!

Aside from being beneficial for school, Band has the option for you to explore other open forums as well.

Whether you're into travel tips, films, books, or anime, there's a Band for you.
